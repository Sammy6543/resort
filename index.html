A Symbol of Excellence

The Great Resort, Bhilwara is a luxury property situated in the Bhilwara district of Rajasthan defining class, sophistication and leisure in a whole new way. The hotel is nestled on Mangrop Road and is in close proximity to Harni Mahadev Mandir. Equipped with modern amenities and facilities, the property strives every single day with an intent of making your stay a memorable experience.
Famous for being the only hotel in Bhilwara with a huge arsenal of services and facilities, The Great Resort will also let you enjoy your tour of Rajasthan to the core. With a plethora of tourist attractions nearby and The Great Resort will let you explore all the places in a very short span of time. You can visit places like Kyara ke Balaji Temple, Mandalgarh Fort and many other tourist places in a jiffy.
